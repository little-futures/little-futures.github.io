Little Futures is a research and consultancy studio from Brian Dell and Tom Critchlow to make futures thinking less abstract and more grounded - to shine a light on what we call “arm’s-length futures”.

With a background in creative stratgy, media & consulting we are experimenting with building a new kind of research partnership and consultancy to help navigate new kinds of futures, little futures.

Our first project is an experimental weekly email to share our thinking and highlight interesting links.
